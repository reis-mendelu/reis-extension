# REIS.mendelu

### A modern, student-built interface for the MENDELU Information System.

**REIS.mendelu is a browser extension that redesigns and streamlines the student experience at Mendel University. We transform the complex Information System into a fast, intuitive, and personalized dashboard so you can focus on your studies, not on searching for information.**

<br>

## The Problem

The MENDELU Information System is powerful, but it's also complex. For students, this often leads to:

-   **Information Overload:** Being presented with dozens of irrelevant links and options.
-   **Too Many Clicks:** Needing to navigate through multiple pages for simple, daily tasks.
-   **Fragmented Data:** Having to check the schedule, document server, and student portal separately to get a full picture.

## Our Solution

REIS.mendelu acts as an intelligent, client-side layer over the existing IS. It doesn't change anything on the university's servers; it simply reorganizes and presents the information in a smarter way, guided by three core principles:

1.  **The 3-Click Rule:** All daily-use, relevant information is accessible within **three clicks**.
2.  **Principle of Absolute Relevance:** We filter and prioritize information for you based on your specific semester, field of study, and faculty.
3.  **Data-Driven Design:** Our improvements are not based on guesses. We use quantitative data to measure and prove that our changes save you time.

## ‚ú® Key Features

*   **Personalized Dashboard:** See your schedule for the day, newly uploaded files, and upcoming deadlines all in one place, right after you log in.
*   **Simplified Interface:** We intelligently hide unnecessary menus and clutter, giving you a clean and focused workspace.
*   **Intelligent Data Linking:** Click on a subject in your schedule and instantly see all related files, teachers, and exam dates without navigating to a different page.
*   **Community Integration:** We provide easy access to resources, events, and contacts relevant to your field of study, in collaboration with student societies.
