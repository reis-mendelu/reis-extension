## Privacy Policy for REIS.mendelu

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

Our commitment to your privacy is simple:

1. We Do Not Collect Personal Data
The REIS.mendelu extension does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, browsing history, or user activity. All operations and data processing happen locally on your computer, within your browser.

2. Website Content
To provide features like in-browser document previews, the extension handles website content (such as course files from is.mendelu.cz). This data is processed locally and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

3. Voluntary Feedback
We use a Google Form to collect voluntary feedback, bug reports, and feature suggestions to improve the extension. The data you submit through this form is used solely for development purposes. The only information collected automatically is your browser's User Agent and the URL of the page where you submitted the feedback, to help us debug issues.

4. No Tracking, No Ads
The extension does not include any analytics, tracking scripts, or advertisements.
